About Us

Welcome to IIMM Ayodhya

The Indian Institute of Materials Management (IIMM) Ayodhya Branch is a distinguished center for excellence in the field of Materials Management, Supply Chain Management, Logistics, and allied disciplines. Established to promote professional education, training, and research in these vital sectors, IIMM Ayodhya works under the esteemed umbrella of IIMM Headquarters, Mumbai, a premier national body dedicated to advancing the profession of materials management across India.

Our Legacy

The Indian Institute of Materials Management (IIMM) has been a cornerstone of professional education and industry collaboration in materials and supply chain management for over five decades. IIMM Ayodhya, as one of its vibrant branches, carries forward this legacy by nurturing talent, building capabilities, and connecting academia with real-world industrial practices.

Situated in the historic city of Ayodhya, our institute combines traditional values with modern education to empower students and professionals to excel in the global business environment.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in materials management education and research, creating professionals who contribute to efficient, sustainable, and globally competitive supply chains.

Our Mission

  • To impart quality education and professional training in materials and supply chain management.
  • To foster strong collaboration between academia and industry.
  • To enhance professional competence through research, seminars, and workshops.
  • To promote ethical practices, innovation, and continuous learning in all areas of operations and logistics.
